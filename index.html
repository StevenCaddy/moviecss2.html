Interstellar

The movie is set in the future. Food is becoming more and more scarce. The only thing that humans are able to grow anymore is corn. The corn is dying. What is leftover of NASA finds a worm-hole that leads to 12 or so possible habitable planets. The main character has to leave his family behind to make sure the human race survives.
